Tim is an urban planner and geographer with an interest in technology, representation, and distance in international development, particularly in Indonesia. He's also data- and compsci-curious, both as a practitioner and a critical observer. He's a recent graduate from the Harvard Graduate School of Design, and will be a doctoral student in development sociology at Cornell University beginning fall 2021 ([Curriculum Vitae](/Ravis_CV.pdf)).

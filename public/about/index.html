About

Tim is an urban planner and geographer with an interest in technology, representation, and value, particularly in the context of international development programming in Indonesia. He’s also data- and compsci-curious, both as a practitioner and a critic. In 2020, he graduated from the Harvard Graduate School of Design, and will begin doctoral studies in development sociology at Cornell University in 2021 (Curriculum Vitae). You can find him on LinkedIn, Academia.edu, Github, Twitter, and Instagram.
